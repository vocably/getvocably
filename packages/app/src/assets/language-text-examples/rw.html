Ibikorwa bya Alice mu Gihugu cy'Ibitangaza

Alice yari yicaye hamwe n'umuvandimwe we ku nkombe y'umugezi, arambirwa kuko nta kintu na kimwe cyo gukora yari afite. Yitegereje ku bitabo by'umuvandimwe we, ariko nta bishushanyo cyangwa ibiganiro biriho. "Kunyurwa no gusoma ibitabo bidafite amashusho ni iki?" yibajije Alice, yibona afashe umwanzuro wo gukangura umutima we w'ubwenge.
