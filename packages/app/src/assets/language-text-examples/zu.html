Amagamelo Ka-Alice Ezweni Lezimanga

U-Alice wayeselahlekelwe umqondo njengoba ehlezi ngasohlangothini lomfula nodadewabo, engasenandaba nokuphila okwakubhalwe encwadini udadewabo ayeyifunda — ngoba izithombe nezingxoxo ezazingenayo zazingathokozisi — uze wazama ukulinda ngaphandle kokuba acabange nge- "hawu, ukuthunga izimbali kungaba nzima ngokwanele. Ingabe kuzobonakala ukufana komuntu onazo?" Ngokuphazima kweso, u-Alice woma waqala ukubheka phezulu, kodwa kwakungcono okumangalisayo okwakude njengephupho. "Oh, yebo!" wayekhuluma ngokwethembeka khona manjalo.

Asazinakanga eceleni komfula, kube sengathi ubuhlungu bomkhandlu onikela nelineni sekumshiyile. Ubhalanta okhanyayo, engathi uyabeka, ukuzula kutholakala kakhulu. Wahamba, wama ohlangothini lomfula, wajika wajuluka. "U-Alice!" umsindo wasekuqaleni umrephuzile, wayezwile kahle. Uqamba nhlebe, wathi, "Oh! into engiyibhalile inzima," wayetshela khona.
