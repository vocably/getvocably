Alice's Adventures in Wonderland

UAlice wayediniwe ngokuhlala nezintlu zakhe ngaphandle kokuba nantoni yokwenza; eneneni, kanye kube kanye, wayejonge encwadini usisi wakhe, kodwa akukho miqolo namfanekiso kuyo, kwaye wanikezela ngokucinga okubini, (njani na isenokuzonwabisa encwadi ngaphandle kwemifanekiso namfanekiso?)

Wayesandoqwalasela (kwamxanyela lokulala), yaza yaqwalaselwa ngumthunzi weRabbit oqongeni, umthandazo ophambili kaRabhibhuthani, owawuphakathi kweRabbit. Emva koko uRabbit waqubula huku, kuye kwavakala xa umviwa wayesondela, naye uAlice wayenelungiselelo lo msebenzi wokuthanda umamkela.
