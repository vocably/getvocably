Alice's Adventures in Wonderland

Alice anali atakhala pampando pamodzi ndi mlongo wake pafupi ndi thambo ndipo sanali kuchita chilichonse. Alinso osangalala ndipo analibwino pafupi kuti achite chilichonse chapadera, bola mlongo wake anali ndi zochita zake, Alice samva bwino kuwerenga mndandanda wa mabuku. Kenako m'mene anali kukumva ngati akugona, mwadzidzidzi adawona kalulu woyera akudutsa.

Koma kalulu uja sinangokhala kalulu wamba, chifukwa uja anali atavala suti, komanso adadzabuka thamba kupita ku dzenje, nthawi imene adaisa mozungulira ndi nthawi yogwira nsinga yake yotondeka. Alice adatsimikiza kuwona chifukwa chake kalulu anali m'bokosi wa mayina ndipo saniyenda yendayi yoyang'ana kwambiri. Koma kukhala odabwa ndi nkhani yake, adatsatizana naye.
